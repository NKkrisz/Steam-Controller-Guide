# Steam Controller Soldering Guide
Follow the [Teardown Guide](./Teardown.md) first to get the board out.

## Tools Needed
- Soldering Iron With Preferably Fine and Knife Tips
- Desoldering Iron / Hot Air Gun / Desoldering Pump
- Tiny Flathead Screwdriver
- Pliers
- Solder Wick
- Solder
- Flux
