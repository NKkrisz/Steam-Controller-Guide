# Disassembling The Steam Controller

## Tools Needed
- Long torx t6 screwdriver
- Pry tools & tweezers / steady hands
- Clean and organized workspace (eg. silicone work mat)
- Soldering iron & desoldering tool + other soldering supplies if swapping joystick module, USB port or other board components

## Tips & Tricks
- Place screws and parts grouped together
- Some parts during disassembly can fall out, work somewhere where they can't get lost easily

## Disassembly
- Magenta: things to look out for
- Red: short screw
- Yellow: long screw
